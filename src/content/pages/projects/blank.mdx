Blank takes a list of restricted url patterns, and intercepts navigation
with a screen requiring you to type a couple words on a digital
typewriter. It gives you time to reflect on if you are doing what you
want to be doing before unleashing the horrors of the web on your poor
prefrontal cortex. Blank is available across Firefox and Chromium
browsers, and comes with a nice tutorial and a bunch of options for
customisation.
