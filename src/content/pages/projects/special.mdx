[SPECial](https://theoryzhenkov.github.io/SPECial/) is a specification standard used across all TheoR projects. I designed it to be LLM-optimized;
It aims to minimise amount of misgathered context agent accumulates and provide a clear, structured way for agents to manage large repositories that don't naively fit in their working memory. It does so through a combined specification-driven and test-driven approach. It defines scopes, domains and assertions, and provies a writing style guides to agents. 

It is public and can be used by anyone. Submit your suggestions to [GitHub](https://github.com/theoryzhenkov/special), or to [proj+special@theor.net](mailto:proj+special@theor.net).
