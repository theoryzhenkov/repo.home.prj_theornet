*Your email, delivered at Posthaste.*

Posthaste is an OSS modular email workstation. As a regular client, it
is very fast, friendly and powerful, but it can also be deployed in
various configurations, subscribed to as a daemon over documented API,
and integrated with agents. I aim to make Posthaste the best email
client in the world.

## 1. I am ...

### 1.1. ... a non-technical person ...

#### 1.1.1 ... who doesn't use email much

Posthaste is fast, pleasant, and nice to look at. You might not have a
need for a better email client, but if you enjoy playing with a new app,
give it a try. All your familiar workflows will be supported by
Posthaste, and who knows — maybe I will have a feature to seduce you.

#### 1.1.2 ... who uses email a lot

You don't need to be a hacker to enjoy Posthaste. Thanks to its
technical prowess, Posthaste is very fast, and it is also fully keyboard
and commands driven. Tag and forward, move to Archive, flag — all of
this just with a few finger taps, no need to touch mouse.

You will find my smart mailboxes exciting — they support complex
queries, including a fast full-text search, and can perform actions on
members. Want to automatically forward invoices to management? No
problem! Have a special directory for family-only? Easy!

### 1.2 ... a technical person ...

#### 1.2.1 ... who doesn't use email much

Maybe I can turn email around for you? By launching Posthaste in a
headless daemon mode on your remote VM you can issue commands to coding
agents through emails. Important notifications can be automatically
transmitted to your TV. Posthaste is fully scriptable and OSS, so you
can even run Doom!

#### 1.2.2 ... who uses email a lot

If all previous sections didn't sound convincing, know that Posthaste is
created by email nerds for email nerds. Full, first-class JMAP support,
offline optimistic writes, smart body & attachment pre-fetching with
learnable online policy. Since Posthaste is split into modular
components, you can write your own backend, runtime or client — make
features you dream of come true, and let me handle the complex parts.
