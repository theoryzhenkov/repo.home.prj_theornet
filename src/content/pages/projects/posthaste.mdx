Posthaste is an OSS email client for users who want to get their email done fast and pleasant.

Posthaste features intuitive query language, smart commands, themes and functionality extensions, local API daemon for agents and scripts, and baseline of all the features you expect from a powerful email solution. It is made to be friendly - available across all platforms, with clear UI/UX; and fast - built on minimal Rust backend over modern JMAP protocol (yeah, it supports IMAP too, bummer). Posthaste is fully open-source, too! You can self-host web version of it, you can turn it into a CLI tool, or have it display your Fediverse feed in a separate window.

Posthaste is fully local and doesn't keep any of your data. Your credentials are stored securily in your OS's secret storage.
