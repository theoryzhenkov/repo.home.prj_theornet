Hello! I'm **Theo R**yzhenkov. This is my [digital
garden](https://maggieappleton.com/garden-history). Welcome!

## 1. Who Is?

I'm a 21 y.o. (and counting) engineer & scientist born in Russia. I did
AI Alignment research at [Palisade](https://palisaderesearch.org/) as a founding member, 
am a creator of [Posthaste](https://posthaste.theor.net/) and an active
member of Rationalist community through FABRIC, LessWrong and other
ventures. I write about software development a lot. I'm trying to get
hired by [Earendil](https://earendil.com/).

- I design and run TTRPGs; Mork Borg, Blades in the Dark as faves.
- I'm in a relational psychoanalysis treatment; My first therapy was at
  11 y.o, in gestalt approach.
- I love dark
  [green](https://www.lesswrong.com/posts/gvNnE6Th594kfdB3z/on-green)
  colors; I enjoy the experience of touching old stones and roads
  or looking at the Earth from planes' windows.
- I paint my nails with wild designs;
- I'm diagnosed with autistic spectrum disorder and ADHD; I take Aripiprazol and Biperiden for my medications! 
- I did professional dance and theater; We performed at [Gogol
  Center](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gogol_Center)!

## 2. Why?

This website is my centralized public presence; in spirit of
[POSSE](https://indieweb.org/POSSE). I want to have one since I believe
that my writing and thoughts can be useful to people and to advance my
own goals.

## 3. How?

This website is built with [Astro](https://astro.build/), MDX-in-git
content, and a small custom ActivityPub/Bluesky service for notes. It is
hosted on my machine on [Hetzner](https://www.hetzner.com/). I collect
statistics with [Medama](https://medama.mintlify.app/introduction). It
is written in TS and deployed declaratively with Nix.

Read about home.theor.net's ontology [here](ontology).
